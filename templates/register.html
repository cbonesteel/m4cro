m4cro is a free web application that helps you pick meals from restaurants in your local area in order to stay in the confines of your diet. You simply enter your desired macronutrient counts and our database and connected APIs {# change later to be more specific #} will recommend a list of meals from local resteraunts that fit your macros and give you estimated travel times for pickup.
