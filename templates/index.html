What is M4cro

M4cro is a free web application that helps you pick meals from resteraunts in your local area in order to stay in the confines of your diet. You simply enter your nutritional information and our database and connected apis {# change later to be more specific #} will generate a list of meals from local resteraunts that fit into your diet and give you estimated travel times to those resteraunts.
